Pork Tenderloin

Home 
Juicy, tender slice, 

            Seared with herbs, a savory bite,

            Flavors warm the soul.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 pork tenderloin (about 1 lb)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • Onion powder 1 teaspoon
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme (or rosemary)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Preheat the oven:
    • Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Prepare the pork tenderloin:
    • Pat the pork tenderloin dry with paper towels. This helps to get a nice sear on the meat.
    • Rub the pork with olive oil. Sprinkle with garlic powder, onion powder, thyme (or rosemary), salt, and pepper, making sure it’s evenly coated.
  3. Sear the pork:
    • Heat a skillet over medium-high heat. Add the olive oil and let it heat up.
    • Once hot, sear the pork tenderloin on all sides (about 2-3 minutes per side) until it’s nicely browned.
  4. Roast the pork:
    • Transfer the skillet with the pork into the preheated oven. Roast for about 20-25 minutes or until the internal temperature reaches 145°F (63°C).
    • If you want extra flavor, you can add 1 tablespoon of butter on top of the pork tenderloin in the oven for the last 5 minutes.
  5. Rest and slice:
    • Remove the pork from the oven and let it rest for 5-10 minutes before slicing. This allows the juices to redistribute.
  6. Serve:
    • Slice into medallions and serve with your favorite side dishes, like mashed potatoes or roasted vegetables.
